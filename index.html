ToppyMicroServices is built for small product teams, technical operators, and founders who need AI-enabled workflows they can still explain, review, and run without a large coordination layer.

We are an independent Estonia-based company focused on operating design, AI evaluation, and conservative software delivery. Public proof artifacts, product pages, and policy pages are part of the company surface, not afterthoughts.
