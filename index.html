About

ToppyMicroServices OÜ is an independent AI & control research studio based in Tallinn, Estonia. We focus on fundamental questions in epistemic stability, AI safety, and financial system modeling.

Research

Our research spans several key areas:

  • Epistemic Stability: Investigating how AI systems maintain consistent and reliable knowledge representations over time.
  • AI Safety: Developing methods and frameworks to ensure AI systems behave safely and align with human values.
  • Financial System Modeling: Analyzing complex interactions and dynamics within financial systems using control theory and computational methods.
